# Layer 2: People Dimension

**Building and Sustaining Digital Government Workforce Capability**

---

## Overview

The People Dimension represents **40% of the GaaS investment framework** and focuses on building the human capability required to design, deliver, and sustain digital government services.

**Core Principle:** Technology unused is technology wasted. People determine adoption, drive innovation, and sustain transformation across political cycles.

---

## People Dimension Documents

### [GaaS Workforce Blueprint](./gaas-workforce-blueprint.md)

**Purpose:** Comprehensive organizational architecture for digital government

**Key Content:**
- Digital government competency framework (10+ role families)
- Skills gap analysis methodology
- Organizational design patterns (multi-disciplinary teams, service owners)
- Career pathways and progression models
- Training and professional development programs
- Change management and adoption strategies
- Leadership capability building

**Target Audience:** CHROs, Organizational Design Leaders, Change Management Teams

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Define 200-500+ digital roles required for transformation
- Build organizational structures that enable whole-of-government service delivery
- Achieve 80%+ digital adoption through effective change management
- Sustain capability across political cycles

---

### [Digital Talent Strategy](./digital-talent-strategy.md)

**Purpose:** Attract, develop, and retain world-class digital talent in public service

**Key Content:**
- Competitive compensation frameworks (targeting 70-85% of market rates)
- Tech company partnership strategies (secondments from Google, Microsoft, Amazon)
- University partnership programs (masters degrees, PhD fellowships, capstone projects)
- Diaspora engagement (repatriating global talent)
- Knowledge transfer mandates (vendor to government capability building)
- 10-15 year indigenization roadmap (government-led by 2035-2040)

**Target Audience:** Recruitment Leaders, Talent Acquisition Teams, CDOs

**Expected Outcomes:**
- Attract 50-200 world-class digital professionals (depending on government size)
- Build internal teams capable of managing $100M-1B digital portfolios
- Reduce vendor dependency from 90% to 40% over 10 years
- Create pipeline of 500-2,000 digitally skilled public servants
- Establish government as attractive tech employer (top 10% of public sector globally)

## Integration with Process Dimension

People and Process work as one integrated engine:

**PEOPLE provide the CAPABILITY:**
- Service designers design citizen journeys
- Platform engineers build and operate infrastructure
- Service managers run day-to-day operations
- Change champions drive adoption
- Data scientists analyze service performance

**PROCESS provides the METHOD:**
- Service design methodologies (co-design, journey mapping)
- Agile delivery frameworks (Scrum, Kanban)
- ITIL service management practices (incident, change, problem management)
- DevOps automation (CI/CD, infrastructure as code)
- TBM cost transparency (service portfolio management)

**TOGETHER they deliver:**
- Services that work (Process quality)
- Services that citizens use (People adoption)
- Services that improve (Continual learning culture)

## Implementation Priorities

### Immediate (Months 1-6)

1. **Recruit Core Leadership**
   - Chief Digital Officer
   - Head of Service Design
   - Head of Platform Engineering
   - Head of People Transformation
   - Head of Service Management

2. **Establish Foundation**
   - Digital Transformation Office (DTO) with 30-50 FTEs
   - Skills gap analysis across government
   - Design compensation frameworks
   - Identify high-potential staff for training

3. **Launch Pilot Training**
   - GSM practitioner certification (first cohort of 20-30)
   - Service design bootcamp (first cohort of 10-15)
   - Change champion training (first cohort of 20-30)

### Short-Term (Months 7-18)

1. **Scale Recruitment**
   - 100-200 external hires (designers, engineers, managers)
   - Tech company secondments (10-20 professionals)
   - University partnerships (recruit from masters programs)
   - Diaspora engagement (identify 50-100 candidates)

2. **Scale Training**
   - 500+ staff through foundational digital government training
   - 200+ GSM certifications
   - 40 service designers trained
   - 30 platform engineers upskilled

3. **Deploy Change Network**
   - 100+ change champions across agencies
   - Quarterly change management workshops
   - Success story campaigns
   - Feedback mechanisms

### Medium-Term (Months 19-36)

1. **Build Indigenous Capability**
   - 60-80% of digital work performed in-house (reduced vendor dependency)
   - Government-led innovation programs
   - Internal mentorship and coaching culture
   - Alumni network for retention

2. **Sustain Transformation**
   - Leadership development program (20-30 leaders/year)
   - Career pathway progression (promotions based on merit)
   - Continual learning culture (500+ conferences, 1,000+ courses)
   - Recognition and awards programs

## Real-World Benchmarks

### Singapore GovTech (Population: 5.6M)

**People Investment:** 35% of transformation budget

**Approach:**
- 3,000+ digital professionals (engineers, designers, product managers)
- Competitive compensation (70-85% of private sector)
- Tech company secondments (Google, Microsoft, Amazon)
- World-class talent from global tech hubs
- 500+ change champions across agencies

**Outcomes:**
- Built internal capability for 90%+ of digital work
- 97% citizen digital service adoption
- Government as attractive employer (turnover <15%)
- Foundation for 1,700+ integrated services

### Estonia (Population: 1.3M)

**People Investment:** 40% of transformation budget

**Approach:**
- National digital literacy program (all ages)
- IT education mandatory from age 7
- Government workforce reskilling (5,000+ employees over 20 years)
- Culture of transparency and trust
- E-Governance Academy for capacity building

**Outcomes:**
- 99% digital adoption by citizens
- 98% government employees digitally capable
- Sustained capability over 25+ years
- Estonia became global thought leader in e-government

### United Arab Emirates (Population: 9.9M)

**People Investment:** 40% of transformation budget

**Approach:**
- Federal Digital Government Strategy 2025
- 5,000+ government employees trained in digital skills
- Golden visa program to attract global talent
- Mandatory innovation training for leadership
- Multi-year capacity building programs

**Outcomes:**
- 90%+ smart services
- Top-10 global e-government ranking
- Government as innovation leader
- 95%+ citizen satisfaction

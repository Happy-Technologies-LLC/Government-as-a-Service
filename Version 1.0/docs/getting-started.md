## <span id="executive-path">üéØ Executive / Decision Maker Path</span>

**Your Journey: Vision ‚Üí Strategic Case ‚Üí Investment Framework ‚Üí Governance**

### Step 1: Understand the Vision (15 minutes)

**Read:** [The GaaS Manifesto](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/gaas-manifesto)

This 6-page vision document explains:
- Why traditional government IT fails (70-80% failure rate)
- The 40-40-20 PPT balance (People-Process-Technology)
- Real-world success stories (Estonia, Singapore, UAE, Denmark)
- The economic case: $10B+ documented savings globally

**Key Takeaway:** Digital government is not about buying technology - it's about building organizational capability.

---

### Step 2: Understand the Operating Model (30 minutes)

**Read:** [Framework Overview](/framework)

Learn the **5-Layer Architecture** + **GaaS Service Value System (GSVS)**:

**Architectural Layers:**
- **Layer 0**: Legal & Policy Foundation (constitutional authority)
- **Layer 1**: Shared Technology Core (national platforms)
- **Layer 2**: People & Process Engine (where transformation happens)
- **Layer 3**: Transparent Service Portfolio (cost visibility)
- **Layer 4**: Unified Citizen Experience (single front-door)

**Process Engine:**
- Mandate ‚Üí Architect ‚Üí Build/Acquire ‚Üí Transition ‚Üí Deliver & Support

**Key Takeaway:** This is an integrated operating model, not a collection of documents.

---

### Step 3: Build the Strategic Case (2 hours)

**Read:** [Layer 3: Portfolio Management Strategic Guide](/layer3-transparent-service-portfolio/portfolio-management-strategic-guide)

This 200+ page guide (formerly the Executive Playbook) provides:
- Investment framework and fiscal models
- Build vs. Buy vs. Partner decisions
- ROI calculations and value measurement
- Phased implementation roadmap (3-5 years)
- Risk mitigation strategies

**Key Questions Answered:**
- How much will this cost? ($50M-500M depending on country size)
- What's the ROI? (2.5:1 to 6:1 over 5 years)
- How long will it take? (18-36 months to operational, 5-7 years to maturity)
- What are the risks? (Political, technical, organizational - with mitigations)

---

### Step 4: Understand the Legal Foundation (1 hour)

**Read:** [Layer 0: Legal & Policy Foundation](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/layer0-overview)

Explore 26+ legislative templates organized by priority:
- **Core (3 essential):** Digital Identity Act, Data Protection Act, E-Government Services Act
- **Enabling (6 recommended):** Cybersecurity Act, Open Data Act, Digital Signature Recognition Act
- **Operational (17 sector-specific):** API Governance, Cloud-First Policy, Service Standards

**Key Takeaway:** You need legal authority before building platforms. Layer 0 comes first.

---

### Step 5: See How It All Works Together (30 minutes)

**Read:** [GaaS Service Value System](/gaas-service-value-system)

Understand the **Service Value Chain** - how policy becomes platforms, platforms enable services, and services create citizen value:

```
Mandate (Layer 0 Policy)
  ‚Üì
Architect (Layer 1 Blueprints + Layer 2 GSM Framework)
  ‚Üì
Build/Acquire (Layer 2 Teams + Agile Delivery)
  ‚Üì
Transition (Layer 2 ITIL Practices)
  ‚Üì
Deliver & Support (Layer 3 Portfolio + Layer 4 Experience)
  ‚Üì
Value Creation (Measured via TBM in Layer 3)
  ‚Üì
Continual Improvement (Feedback to Layer 2 Teams)
```

**Key Takeaway:** Every layer has a specific role in the value chain. All must work together.

### Step 1: Understand How Work Flows (30 minutes)

**Read:** [GaaS Service Value System](/gaas-service-value-system)

Before diving into technical blueprints, understand the **process engine** that orchestrates work across layers:

- **Service Value Chain:** Mandate ‚Üí Architect ‚Üí Build/Acquire ‚Üí Transition ‚Üí Deliver
- **28 GSM Practices:** How work gets coordinated (Incident, Change, Release Management)
- **Cross-Layer Dependencies:** Which layer provides what capability

**Key Takeaway:** You're not just building technology - you're designing a system where people, processes, and technology work as one.

---

### Step 2: Study the Shared Technology Core (2-3 hours)

**Read:** [Layer 1: Shared Technology Core Overview](/layer1-shared-technology-core/layer1-overview)

Understand the **"Build Once, Use Everywhere"** philosophy:

**Core Platforms:**
- National Digital Identity (authentication & authorization)
- API Gateway & Data Exchange (interoperability)
- Cloud Infrastructure (compute, storage, network)
- Analytics & AI Platform (insights & automation)
- National Notification System (SMS, email, push)
- Cybersecurity Operations Center (SOC)

**Architecture Principles:**
- API-first design
- Zero-trust security
- Cloud-native (Kubernetes, serverless)
- Open standards (OAuth 2.0, OIDC, SAML, REST/GraphQL)

**Deep-Dive Blueprints:**
- [Process & Service Management Architecture](/layer1-shared-technology-core/volume-2-process-service-management-architecture)
- [Technology & Platform Architecture](/layer1-shared-technology-core/volume-3-technology-platform-architecture)

---

### Step 3: Understand the Operating Layer (2-3 hours)

**Read:** [Layer 2: People & Process Engine Overview](/layer2-people-process-engine/layer2-overview)

This is the **40-40-20 PPT balance** in action:

**People Dimension (40%):**
- [GaaS Workforce Blueprint](/layer2-people-process-engine/people/gaas-workforce-blueprint) - 200-500+ digital roles
- [Digital Talent Strategy](/layer2-people-process-engine/people/digital-talent-strategy) - Recruit and retain

**Process Dimension (40%):**
- 10 Process Playbooks covering:
  - Strategy & Design: Digital Identity, Co-Design, Service Portfolio
  - Build & Deploy: API Gateway, Agile Delivery
  - Secure & Govern: Zero Trust, Cybersecurity, Data Governance
  - Operate & Improve: GSM Implementation, Change Management

**Technology Dimension (20%):**
- Enablers from Layer 1 platforms

**Key Takeaway:** Your architecture must support people and processes, not just run software.

---

### Step 4: Explore Implementation Playbooks (3-4 hours)

**Browse:** [Layer 2 Process Playbooks](/layer2-people-process-engine/processes/process-digital-identity)

Each playbook provides 18-30 month implementation roadmaps:

**Technical Playbooks:**
- [Process: Digital Identity](/layer2-people-process-engine/processes/process-digital-identity) - NIST 800-63, eIDAS compliance
- [Process: API Gateway](/layer2-people-process-engine/processes/process-api-gateway) - Kong, Apigee, AWS API Gateway patterns
- [Process: Zero Trust Migration](/layer2-people-process-engine/processes/process-zero-trust) - NIST 800-207 implementation

**Operational Playbooks:**
- [Process: GSM Implementation](/layer2-people-process-engine/processes/process-gsm-implementation) - ITIL v4 for government
- [Process: Service Portfolio & Cost Management](/layer2-people-process-engine/processes/process-service-portfolio-cost) - TBM framework

**Key Takeaway:** These aren't theoretical guides - they're battle-tested playbooks from Estonia, Singapore, UAE.

---

### Step 5: Study Integration Patterns (1 hour)

**Read:** Layer overview "Integration with Other Layers" sections:

- [Layer 1 Integration](/layer1-shared-technology-core/layer1-overview#integration-with-other-layers)
- [Layer 2 Integration](/layer2-people-process-engine/layer2-overview#integration-with-other-layers)
- [Layer 3 Integration](/layer3-transparent-service-portfolio/layer3-overview#integration-with-other-layers)

**Key Integration Points:**
- Layer 0 provides legal authority for data sharing ‚Üí Layer 1 implements technical controls
- Layer 1 provides APIs ‚Üí Layer 2 teams consume and operate them
- Layer 2 produces services ‚Üí Layer 3 manages their portfolio and costs
- Layer 3 exposes services ‚Üí Layer 4 presents unified citizen experience

## <span id="citizen-path">üë• Citizen / Advocate Path</span>

**Your Journey: Vision ‚Üí Citizen Experience ‚Üí Co-Design ‚Üí Community**

### Step 1: Understand the Vision (15 minutes)

**Read:** [The GaaS Manifesto](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/gaas-manifesto)

Learn what digital government means for you:
- **Contactless:** Government services available 24/7 on your phone
- **Intelligent:** AI-powered services that anticipate your needs
- **Automated:** Instant responses, no waiting in queues
- **Persistent:** Government remembers you (securely)
- **Secure:** Your privacy and data protected

**Real-World Examples:**
- **Estonia:** 99% of services online, 15 minutes average interaction time per year
- **Singapore:** 97% adoption, 4M+ users, 95% satisfaction
- **Denmark:** 90% digital adoption, mandatory digital post system

---

### Step 2: Explore the Citizen Experience (30 minutes)

**Read:** [Layer 4: Unified Citizen Experience](/layer4-unified-citizen-experience/layer4-overview)

Discover what a digital government looks like:

**Unified Portal:**
- Single login for all government services (digital identity)
- Personalized dashboard showing your services, benefits, obligations
- Proactive notifications (e.g., "Your passport expires in 60 days")

**Once-Only Principle:**
- Never re-enter data government already has
- Pre-filled forms with your information
- Seamless data sharing (with your consent)

**Omnichannel Access:**
- Web portal, mobile app, voice assistant, in-person kiosks
- Same experience across all channels
- Assisted digital support for those who need help

---

### Step 3: Understand Your Rights and Protections (30 minutes)

**Browse:** [Layer 0: Legal & Policy Foundation](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/layer0-overview)

Learn about the laws that protect you:

**Data Protection:**
- [Data Protection & Privacy Act](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/data-protection-privacy-act) - Your data rights
- [Data Sharing Framework](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/data-sharing-framework) - How government shares data (with consent)

**Transparency:**
- [Open Data & Transparency Act](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/open-data-transparency-act) - Access to government data
- [Algorithmic Accountability Policy](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/algorithmic-accountability-policy) - AI transparency

**Digital Rights:**
- [Digital Identity Act](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/digital-identity-act) - Your digital identity rights
- [E-Government Services Act](/layer0-legal-policy-foundation/e-government-services-act) - Right to digital services

---

### Step 4: Participate in Co-Design (1 hour)

**Read:** [Community Platform Overview](/layer4-unified-citizen-experience/community-platform-overview)

Your voice matters in shaping digital government:

**Ways to Participate:**
- **User Testing:** Test new services before public launch
- **Feedback Forums:** Share your experience with existing services
- **Co-Design Workshops:** Help design services that meet your needs
- **Citizen Advisory Councils:** Represent citizen perspective in governance

**Real-World Example:**
Singapore's LifeSG app was co-designed with 5,000+ citizens through workshops, surveys, and usability testing over 18 months.

---

### Step 5: Advocate for Better Services (30 minutes)

- "Government should implement the **Once-Only Principle** so I don't re-enter data"
- "We need **TBM cost transparency** - show me how my taxes are spent on digital services"

**Share Success Stories:**
- Estonia saves ‚Ç¨100M/year through digital government
